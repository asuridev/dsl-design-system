Sagas del sistema
CheckoutSaga
Disparador: OrderPlaced (orders)

Coordinates the checkout process across inventory, payments, orders and delivery. Reserves stock, processes payment, confirms the order and triggers home delivery. Compensates by releasing reserved stock and refunding the card charge if any step fails.

catalog
core

Manages the product catalog: categories, product details, images and pricing. It is the single authoritative source for all product data and prices in the platform.

Aggregates: Product, Category

orders
core

Manages the full order lifecycle from cart creation and checkout through payment confirmation, delivery tracking and order completion. Validates prices against catalog via HTTP at checkout (monetary snapshot). Maintains a local read model of customer addresses fed by CustomerAddressUpdated events from the customers BC (designer chose LRM for delivery address — non-monetary identity data).

Aggregates: Order, Cart

RevisiónDiagramasAPI RESTEventos
payments
supporting

Processes and records payments for orders. Supports card payments via an external payment gateway and cash-on-delivery payments recorded when the courier collects the cash. Handles refunds for cancelled confirmed orders charged by card.

Aggregates: Payment

inventory
supporting

Tracks real-time stock levels for each active product. Creates a StockItem when a product is activated and permanently closes it when the product is discontinued. Reserves stock during the CheckoutSaga and releases it as compensation if payment fails.

Aggregates: StockItem

Diseño táctico pendiente
delivery
supporting

Manages home delivery orders using the company's own courier fleet. Handles the full delivery lifecycle from assignment to completion. Records cash-on-delivery events for the payments BC.

Aggregates: DeliveryOrder, Courier

Diseño táctico pendiente
customers
supporting

Manages customer accounts and their delivery addresses. Publishes CustomerAddressUpdated events consumed by orders to maintain a local read model of delivery addresses for order snapshots.

Aggregates: Customer

notifications
generic

Delivers transactional notifications to customers (email, SMS, push) for key order lifecycle events: order confirmed, order cancelled, delivery completed. Fully delegates sending to an external notification provider. Generic domain — no competitive advantage; no business logic beyond template selection and dispatch.

Aggregates: Notification
